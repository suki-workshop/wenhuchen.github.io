About me

I'm a first-year PHD student in Computer Science Department of University of California, Santa Barbara and I'm really hornored to be supervised by both Prof. William Wang and Prof. Xifeng Yan. Before that, I spent a wonderful half year in Natural Language Computing Group in Microsoft Research, Asia from Feb. 2017 to Sep. 2017, there I worked with Dr. Shujie Liu, Dr. Mu Li and Dr. Ming Zhou. My research intests lie in natural language processing and machine learning, more specifically, I focuse on research tasks like machine translation, knolwedge base reasoning, semantic parsing and image captioning and video captioning.
